What it is

A Kingdom Operating System for the Seeker.

Not another reading app. 633k fuses Logos-grade depth with consumer-grade design — tap any verse for doctrinally-reviewed AI context and the original languages, follow every connection through a personal knowledge-graph of Scripture, and walk it together with your church.

Read · Journal · Memorize
